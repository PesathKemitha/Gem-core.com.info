What Are Gems?

Gemstones are rare minerals formed deep inside the Earth. They're admired for their color, clarity, rarity, and strength. From ancient times to modern luxury, gems continue to captivate and inspire.

How Are Gems Formed?

Gems form deep underground under extreme heat and pressure. Some are born from magma flows, others from hydrothermal processes. Over millions of years, these elements crystalize into the stunning stones we admire today.

How Do People Use Gems?

  • Jewelry: Worn in rings, necklaces, and crowns.
  • Spiritual Use: Some believe they hold healing power.
  • Industry: Diamonds are used in tools and lasers.
  • Collections: Rare gems are collected as treasures.
